However, most people are now happy with how the Targaryens have been portrayed and want to know more about the actors that bring them to life including how much money they are worth.

    9 How Much Is Emma D'Arcy Worth?

    A non-binary actor who uses they/them pronouns, Emma D’Arcy seems poised to become one of the most famous actors of their generation.

    Still very young in their career, D’Arcy previously starred in the shows Wanderlust and Truth Seekers but now they are best known for bringing Rhaenyra Targaryen to life in House of the Dragon.

    As of the time of this writing, the most credible financial publications like Forbes and celebritynetworth.com haven’t reported on Emma D’Arcy’s net worth yet. That said, according to famouspeopletoday.com, D’Arcy is currently worth $500,000 which is a number that is sure to grow.

    8 How Much Is Harry Lloyd Worth?

    An extremely memorable part of Game of Thrones’ first season, Harry Lloyd played Viserys Targaryen, the cruel brother of Daenerys Targaryen. An accomplished television and film actor, Lloyd has appeared in a slew of other projects including series like Manhattan, Legion, and Brave New World.

    Just like Emma D’Arcy, Forbes and celebritynetworth.com have both ignored Harry Lloyd as of the time of this writing. According to celebworth.net, Lloyd’s current net worth is $2 million and while that website is less credible, that report seemingly gives readers a ballpark idea of Harry’s fortune.

    7 How Much Is Milly Alcock Worth?

    A fascinating young actor, Milly Alcock rose to international fame due to her tenure playing a teenage version of Rhaenyra Targaryen. Previously the star of the Australian show Upright, Alcock also has shown up in other shows like Reckoning and Netflix’s Pine Gap.

    Related: How Milly Alcock Really Became A Targaryen In House Of The Dragon

    After House of the Dragon jumped forward in time, Emma D'Arcy took over the role that Milly Alcock first introduced to viewers. Assuming that Alcock never returns to House of the Dragon again, she was so great in the role that she almost certainly will get many opportunities.

    According to celebritynetworth.com, Milly Alcock currently has a $2 million net worth.

    6 How Much Is Olivia Cooke Worth?

    Just like Emma D'Arcy, Olivia Cooke inherited her House of the Dragon role from a younger actor, the extremely talented Emily Carey. Not a Targaryen by birth, Cooke's character Alicent Hightower married into the family when she became King Viserys I's Queen.

    Previously a star of the show Bates Motel and movies like Me and Earl and the Dying Girl as well as Sound of Metal, Olivia Cooke has already accomplished a lot in her acting career. With that in mind, any House of the Dragon fan should be excited that Cooke is a big part of the show.

    Even though Olivia Cooke already has an impressive career, she is worth $2 million as of this writing according to celebritynetworth.com, just like Milly Alcock whose career is still taking off.

    5 How Much Is Eve Best Worth?

    The epitome of an extremely talented character actor, Eve Best may not be a household name but she earns the respect of anyone who sees her work. Previously a star of series like Nurse Jackie and The Honourable Woman, Best also had a role in The King's Speech.

    Cast as House of the Dragon's Rhaenys Targaryen, Eve Best plays the cousin of the King who was passed over for the throne. Clearly a formidable character, Best seems perfectly cast to play Rhaenys.

    According to celebritynetworth.com, Eve Best has a $3 million fortune as of this writing.

    4 How Much Is Paddy Considine Worth?

    Just like Eve Best, Paddy Considine has long been the kind of actor who makes every project he appears in better. A two-time winner at the British Academy Film Awards, Considine has appeared in so many films and shows that trying to list his career highlights would take up too much room.

    Perfectly cast as House of the Dragon's King Viserys I Targaryen, Paddy Considine has managed to make the character feel fully fleshed out.

    According to celebritynetworth.com, Paddy Considine has a $4 million fortune as of this writing.

    3 How Much Is Matt Smith Worth?

    Clearly the kind of actor that everyone wants to work with, Matt Smith has starred in several massively successful series. Forever known for playing the eleventh incarnation of Doctor Who, Smith also played Prince Phillip in The Crown, and now he is starring in House of the Dragon.

    Related: Who Was Matt Smith Before He Became Daemon Targaryen?

    Even though Matt Smith plays the younger brother of the king, his portrayal of Prince Daemon Targaryen has ensured the character steals every scene he appears in.

    According to celebritynetworth.com, Matt Smith has a current net worth of $9 million.

    2 How Much Is Kit Harington Worth?

    When Game of Thrones came to an end, there were a few actors that everyone believed had the potential to become superstars. Definitely one of the people on that list Kit Harington even was tapped to join the Marvel Cinematic Universe when he debuted in Eternals.

    When the character best known as Jon Snow was introduced, everyone thought he was Eddard Stark's bastard child. However, it was eventually revealed that Snow was given the name Aegon Targaryen at birth as his biological parents were Rhaegar Targaryen and Lyanna Stark.

    According to celebritynetworth.com, Kit Harington has an impressive $14 million fortune as of this writing.

    1 How Much Is Emilia Clarke Worth?

    Virtually unknown when she was hired to star in Game of Thrones, Emilia Clarke is now a huge star. Just like Kit Harrington before her, Clarke is set to join the MCU in the upcoming series Secret Invasion and she has starred in Terminator and Star Wars movies.

    Thanks to Emilia Clarke's stellar portrayal of the character, Daenerys Targaryen became a household name in much of the world during the height of Game of Thrones' popularity.
