    Drake, a global music sensation, spends a lot on his clothes, as a rapper and vocalist known for wearing luxury labels and exclusive collaborations.

    Drake has created a name for himself in the music industry and is currently worth $260 million. He mixes in trendy and inexpensive pieces but never hesitates to spend big on unique outfits.

    Drake has donned some fantastic costumes on stage and off, from tens of thousands of dollars in custom suits to exotic cowhide ensembles costing more than a luxury car. The initial sale and resale prices of Drake's rarest pieces will determine how much his flexes cost.

    Join us as we count down Drake's most expensive outfits to get a taste of high fashion's outrageous prices.

    9 Hermès Birkin Bag Customized As A Briefcase: $40,000

    Even Drake's trinkets are expensive, like his Hermès Birkin bag that he had crafted into a clutch in a briefcase style. Birkin bags start at about $40,000, but pieces made just for you can cost even more.

    Drake took the famous Hermès Birkin tote and turned it into a sleek leather clutch bag that looks like a suitcase and has a long strap across the body. Drake carries his favorite Birkin bag everywhere, from the airport to a music video set, and he gave the ultimate prestigious purse a stylish twist.

    8 Amiri Snakeskin Jacket: $45,000

    Drake enjoys wearing leather, and among his favorite clothing lines is Amiri, renowned for fusing LA street wear with a rocker style.

    Drake has been pictured wearing various priceless Amiri items, including a $45,000 snakeskin leather jacket from the designer's runway line. The pricey jacket, crafted from genuine snakeskin with a cool, muted green tint, perfectly captures Drake's love of exotic leather and advanced style.

    7 Tom Ford Crocodile Leather Jacket: $55,000

    Drake stepped out in style during New York Fashion Week in 2021, in a leather jacket with a cropped hem by Tom Ford. Not just any leather, though; the arms and top part of this jacket were covered in scaled crocodile leather, which had a shiny finish.

    The retail cost of this exotic jacket, made from genuine crocodile leather and featuring Tom Ford's distinctive, sensual style, is a staggering $55,000. Drake finished the look as usual with frosty gems to go with the opulent croc leather.

    6 Louis Vuitton Monogram Mink Bomber Jacket: $70,000

    During a festival performance in 2022, Drake made an appearance supported by Louis Vuitton, donning a unique bomber jacket adorned with the LV emblem. This exceptional jacket was meticulously manufactured with genuine mink fur.

    Priced at over $70,000, this remarkably scarce runway item from Louis Vuitton is crafted with high-quality materials, featuring the renowned LV monogram pattern on the mink fabric exterior. Drake's customized Louis Vuitton monogram mink bomber jacket exudes opulence on all levels, characterized by its truncated silhouette and adorned with a black shearling collar.

    5 Diamond Encrusted Rolex: $75,000

    Drake is known for wearing flashy designer watches, such as a 40mm Rolex Date – entirely covered in diamonds that were carefully placed in a diagonal pattern. These timepieces go well with Drake's expensive outfits.

    Over 50 carats of diamonds were hand set on a sturdy 18k white gold wristwatch (by Jason of Beverly Hills) to create this jewel-encrusted, iced out Rolex. The complete watch, adorned with magnificent cut diamonds, is valued at over $75,000.

    Drake always turns to his diamond-paved Rolex when he wants to increase the richness level of his daily looks.

    4 Dior Floral Suit At The Billboard Awards: $95,000

    Drake commissioned a custom white Dior Homme suit for his monumental return appearance at the 2021 Billboard Music Awards. The two-piece suit featured meticulous embroidery of light-blue flowers and Dior's trademark bee emblem.

    With intricate handmade details and building from Dior's bespoke atelier, insiders in the fashion industry estimate that this floral suit rang up at approximately $95,000. Drake completed his high fashion slash couture ensemble with a unique Dior Oblique Saddle purse and flashy Chrome Hearts sunglasses.

    3 Custom Chrome Hearts Denim Ensemble: $100,000

    In 2020, the LA-based label Chrome Hearts crafted Drake a one-of-a-kind denim jumpsuit with embroidered Chrome Hearts logos. Chrome Hearts' characteristic dagger and cross graphics adorned the handcrafted denim jacket, pants, and cap that matched perfectly.

    Drake's one-of-a-kind outfit, featuring high-end Japanese denim clothes and an absurd number of hand stitched decorations, likely cost him more than $100,000. The pricey and exclusive label, known for its limited edition, gothic-inspired accessories and clothing, created everything especially for him.

    2 Custom Chrome Hearts Leather Jacket: $150,000

    Drake asked the Los Angeles company Chrome Hearts to make a unique black leather jacket tailored to his taste. Daggers and crosses made of genuine sterling silver emblematic of Chrome Hearts were added to their backs and sleeves as decorations.

    The jacket also included precious metal hardware, including elaborate silver spike studs. This unique sculpture cost about $150,000 and had Drake's name inscribed in silver and handmade unique metal accents.

    The exorbitant price reflects the uniqueness and exclusivity of this jacket, which Chrome Hearts' skilled jewelers and leather workers created just for Drake.

    1 Custom OVO x Murakami Varsity Jacket: $200,000

    In 2015, Drake teamed up with Takashi Murakami, the famous Japanese artist, and created a custom-made OVO varsity jacket wherein Drake showcased his love for high-end exclusive collaboration. Murakami painted the black leather jacket himself in his trademark colorful flower motif, covering the whole back and arms of the jacket in vivid, multicolored flowers.

    Moreover, as the flower designs are one of Murakami's most famous works and very seldom painted onto clothing, that was the perfect pairing for the collaboration between the two masters. The one-of-a-kind collaborative piece between Drake, Murakami, and Louis Vuitton was most likely sold at around $200,000, considering the artist hand-painted each jacket.

    At the Toronto International Film Festival 2018, Drake wore a custom OVO x Murakami varsity jacket, showing the merging of street wear and fine art, representing the avant-garde fashion sense of Drake and how he would team up with different hip-hop artists.
