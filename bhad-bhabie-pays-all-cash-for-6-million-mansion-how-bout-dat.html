    Bhad Bhabie managed to torpedo her way into the spotlight during a 2017 appearance on Dr. Phil with just six words: "Cash me outside, how bout dah?" Now, thanks to some savvy business moves and a mystifying ability to outlive the viral meme that started her career —Bhad Bhabi is one of the richest self-made teenagers around —and she just paid cash for a bougie $6 million Florida mansion.

    RELATED: Shaquille O'Neal Sells Florida Home For $11M, Half Of The Asking Price After 3 Years

    Bhad Bhabie, whose real name is Danielle Bregoli, made headlines last year after she racked up $1 million in her first six hours on OnlyFans, including $757,526.08 from subscriptions, $267,675 from DM payments, and $5,502.35 in tips.

    “Not bad for 6 hours,” she wrote. “We broke the f--k out of that onlyfans record.”

    Despite being 19-years-old, Bhabie is already experienced with real estate and her newfound tax bracket. Like most celebrities, she’s got a mansion in Los Angeles, but she makes sure to spend at least six months a year in Boca Raton to remain a Florida resident. And now, she’s got a new pad in the sunshine state because she’s “not about to pay these [California] taxes.”

    As per The Real Deal, the singer recently scored a one-acre estate from Tampa Bay Buccaneers linebacker Jason Pierre-Paul, who purchased the property in 2017 for $2.3 million. The NFL star gave the space a modern makeover, and then flipped it to Bhad Bhabie for a cool $6 million.

    Bhabie, who became the youngest woman to ever land on the “Billboard Hot 100” chart, is the new owner of the monochromatic mansion, which features seven bedrooms and seven bathrooms in nearly 9,300 square feet of living space. The property features numerous high-end touches like porcelain tile, Lutron lighting, and a Sonos smart-home. Amenities include a massive palm tree-lined backyard, a  swimming pool, and a five-car garage to store her growing luxury car collection.

    The Boca Raton mansion also features a two-story guesthouse complete with its own kitchen.

    Bhad Bhabie has managed to turn her viral fame into a fortune estimated to be as high as eight figures. These days she has a few income streams in addition to her OnlyFans, including a$100,000 fee for brand endorsements. Plus, she has a $1 million record deal, and she charges north of $40,000 for a single meet-and-greet session.
