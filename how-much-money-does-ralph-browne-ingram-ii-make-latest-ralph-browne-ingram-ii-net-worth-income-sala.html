    Chip Ingram is a Christian preacher, author, and orator who was born in 1954. His educational programmes have been aired on radio and television throughout the globe. According to Radio World, Ingram’s shows are broadcast weekly on 850 radio stations throughout the United States. He is the presenter of Sirius XM Radio’s Living on the Edge with Chip Ingram, a teaching ministry. Ingram’s programming reached 34 million homes through television in 2011 via DirectTV and FamilyNet. According to National Religious Broadcasters, Ingram’s broadcasts have been extensively broadcast across Russian-speaking nations and the Arab East (NRB). According to the NRB, Ingram’s shows were aired 12 times weekly to millions of Arab homes in 2011.
