    Late American Musician and songwriter Eddie Van Halen passed away on October 6, 2020. He suffered from health issues related to smoking, alcoholism, and acrobatic stage performances since he was 12-years-old. Eddie got married to actress and TV personality Valerie Bertinelli in 1981. They later divorced in 2007.

    The American-Dutch musician later married pro wrestler and stunt woman Janie Liszewski in 2009. The couple didn't have children, but they shared a tiny lovely little Pomeranian dog named Kody, whom they both loved so much. After his death, Janie, Eddie's wife, made headlines for going through a difficult phase because of her husband's fate. 2021 was a different year for Janie Van Halen; she had many things on hand and fought hard like a warrior to reclaim her life back.

    8 She's Willing To Get Back To Ballroom Dance Competitions

    One year after Eddie Van Halen passed away, Janie announced on Instagram that she's willing to get back to ballroom dance competitions, although not ready. That comes after a 2-year hiatus, little practice, and a whole lot of nerves, as Liszewski claimed. Janie will compete in the Hollywood DanceSport Championships on October 28 as part of the American SmoothSingle and Multi Dances.

    7 Janie Is Sad Her Husband And Brother Won't Be Present To Cheer For Her

    Janie expressed her sadness and bittersweet feeling, which she described as an understatement, because her most prominent supporters, including her husband, brother, and mother, won't be present at her competition to cheer for her. However, Van Halen said that she knew the spirits of her loved ones would be watching from above and that she would do her best to make them proud.

    6 Janie Van Halen Continues To Care For Kody

    Janie is caring for her little dog Kody Van Halen. The latter was very close to Eddie and appeared with him on several occasions and photoshoots. Kody is of the Pomeranian breed, a super cute dog that provided necessary emotional support for Eddie and Janie. The tiny dog turned into an internet sensation and has a Facebook page with more than 13,000 followers. Janie operates the page and posts constant updates about little star Kody's activities, interviews, and news.

    5 She Posted A New Pic As A Teacher

    On September 29, Janie posted a bright, smiling, and happy picture of herself on Instagram and Hash-tagged it with 2021, Teacher, Private School, Picture Day, Mrs. Janie, adding that it's been decades since her last school picture. Van Halen received outpouring support from friends and fans who cheered on Janie for starting to live her life again and insist on working and achieving despite all that she's gone through.

    4 Janie Is Looking After Her Health

    Apart from being a dancer and a pro wrestler, Janie Van Halen has recently taken care of her health and well-being. Last month, she took to Instagram and posted a picture of herself with a rejuvenating face mask for her skin. Janie also ensures to eat healthy food and hit the gym regularly. After her husband's death, Janie underwent mental therapy to try and recover after the devastating events that shook her life.

    3 Janie Van Halen Is Working As A Party DJ

    In addition to participating in dance competitions, teaching at school, being a pro wrestler, and a stunt woman, Janie Van Halen also works as a party DJ. She spent the year DJ-ing live on Instagram and Facebook and in several events with her ballroom dance partner Louie Martinez. Just recently, the duet DJ-ed at their back-to-school BBQ.

    2 She Lost Her Mother In 2020

    As if it wasn't enough for her to lose her husband Eddie and brother Tom, Janie also lost her mother last November. Van Halen mourned her mom in an Instagram post, and she's been posting about how much she loves her since then. Janie describes her mother as her rock and the person who knew her best. In August, she wished her mother a happy birthday in heaven and told her that not a day goes that she doesn't think about her.

    1 Janie Van Halen's Net Worth Amounts To $500,000

    According to Legit, Janie Van Halen has a net worth of $500,000. She's an entrepreneur who also works as a ballroom dancer, stunt woman, party DJ, teacher, and pro wrestler. Janie played roles in A Man Apart, Bubble Boy, and Dusk Till Dawn. In addition to being the wife of the late musician and songwriter Eddie Van Halen, all of those professions contributed to Janie's wealth.
