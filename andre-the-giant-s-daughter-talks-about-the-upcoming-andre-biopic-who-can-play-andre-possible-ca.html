    Andre The Giants Daughter Talks About The Upcoming Andre Biopic, Who Can Play Andre?, Possible Ca

    21 Aug 2024, 00:00

    WrestleZone Radio is proud to present this exclusive look at the newly announced Andre the Giant movie based off of Lion Forge Comic’s Andre The Giant: Closer To Heaven graphic novel.

    You can find some transcribed quotes from the daughter of Andre the Giant, Robin Christensen-Roussimoff, about the upcoming film below.

    On who could play her father, Andre the Giant, in this upcoming film:

    Robin: It will be a live action movie. Who’s going to play my Dad? I think I’ll leave that to the professionals. We are going to have to get a character actor, obviously. I don’t know how they are going to do it exactly. They might do somebody that is tall enough for visual and then have someone do the voice. I don’t know if it’s going to be digital or practical make-up. It will be interesting. He had such a unique look. He had such a unique voice. They are really going to need to pull out a lot of special effects for this. 

    On potential cameos from wrestlers Andre worked with or cast members from The Princess Bride:

    Robin: I would say the chances are probably pretty high. The Princess Bride was such a big thing in his life and his career. They are going to have to have other wrestlers depicted in this. I would say there is a pretty good chance. I am sure they will find people to portray them. I don’t know that it will actually be them. 

    On who she would like to see direct the movie:

    Robin: My personal choice, in my geekdom, is Joss Whedon. He’s just got such an interesting view of things that I think he could make it a lot of fun. That’s my personal pick but I don’t know who they have in mind. 

    On when the movie could be in theaters:

    Robin: It’s going to be a couple of years. They are still finalizing the script and the director. They still have to cast. It’s going to be a couple of years.
