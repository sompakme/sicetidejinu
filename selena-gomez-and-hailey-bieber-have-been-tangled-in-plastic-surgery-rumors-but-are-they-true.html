    Selena Gomez is an accomplished actress and producer of TV and film, a music superstar, and the CEO of a successful makeup line called Rare Beauty. Selena has amassed a large fan base thanks partly to her talent, advocacy, struggles with mental and physical illness, and down-to-earth personality. While Gomez has often caused a stir with her entertainment projects, she has also made headlines for other things, such as her supposed rivalry with her ex-boyfriend's (Justin Bieber) wife, Hailey Bieber.

    Like Selena Gomez, Hailey Bieber is also a highly accomplished woman in her own right. She is the creator of a makeup and skincare brand called Rhode Skin and is one of the world's most sought-after models, having modeled for famed designers such as Ralph Lauren and Tommy Hilfiger. Given Gomez's and Beiber's influence and celebrity, people often ask if the two striking beauties have had any plastic surgery.

    Has Selena Gomez Ever Had Plastic Surgery?

    Selena Gomez was listed in People Magazine's 2016 issue as one of the world's Most Beautiful Women. Her legion of fans and admirers would undoubtedly agree as they've helped make Selena the most-followed female celebrity on Instagram.

    Despite her looks, Gomez is surprisingly humble about it and has even shockingly expressed on more than one occasion that she is often critical of her physical appearance.

    In an interview with Harpar's Bazaar magazine, Selena Gomez stated that one of the reasons she created her makeup brand Rare Beauty was "To promote self-acceptance and create a space in the industry that supports mental well-being across age, gender identities, sexual orientations, race, culture, physical and mental abilities, and perspectives."

    Given Selena's celebrity status and contribution to the makeup industry, women naturally want to know all of her beauty secrets to apply them to themselves; this is a significant reason why many want to know if the star has ever undergone plastic surgery.

    While Gomez once joked about receiving Botox, the "Calm Down" singer has never admitted to having it or any other work done to her face.

    Gomez has also advocated for body positivity and acceptance after being the victim of online bullies who've made cruel and unjust remarks about her weight. She told Billboard magazine several years ago that despite her flaws, such as the scar left by her kidney transplant, she had no desire to remove it through plastic surgery. Still, the star was adamant that there is nothing wrong with anyone who chooses to have cosmetic surgeries done.

    Of course, people have still wondered if Selena has ever done any surgery to enhance her natural beauty.

    Some of the most prevalent speculations are that Gomez got fox eye surgery to make her eyes appear almond in shape, along with breast implants and rhinoplasty, all of which the singer has declined to comment on. However, fans argue that makeup, lighting, and photo editing can drastically alter a person's appearance.

    Additionally, the Only Murders in the Building actress has stated that the medications she takes to treat her lupus have contributed to fluctuations in weight, which would account for many of the physical changes to her body.

    Has Hailey Bieber Ever Had Plastic Surgery?

    Model Hailey Bieber is undoubtedly one of the most sought-after and famous fashion models and beauty influencers today. In fact, the star was named one of Time Magazine's Top100 Next People to Watch of 2023.

    Although Hailey is often in the news for her relationship with her husband, Justin Bieber (such as the time Justin had meltdown when Hailey won against him in an arcade game) she has managed to forge a name for herself outside her mega-famous husband.

    One of the questions that leaves fans curious is if the model has ever had any plastic surgery done. According to Hailey Bieber, she has never had any cosmetic surgeries, at least not to her face.

    In an article by The Standard, the Rhode Beauty founder addressed the plastic surgery rumors to her millions of followers on Instagram. The image in question that she was responding to was posted by a fan, which showed a side-by-side comparison of Hailey when she was a teenager and an adult. Hailey commented:

    "Stop using pics that makeup artists edit! This photo on the right is NOT what I look like. I've never touched my face...If you're gonna sit around and compare me at 13 and then me at 23, at least use a natural photo that wasn't edited so crazy."

    Although Hailey and many other celebrities have denied using plastic surgery, it hasn't stopped an overzealous public from questioning their denials.

    For example, some fans still think Hailey Bieber has undergone rhinoplasty, cheek, jawline, and chip lip fillers. Whomever one chooses to believe, it is essential to note that speculation over a celebrity's looks can be a form of bullying and also cause a sullied reputation, as plastic surgery is still considered taboo despite how widespread it has become in society.

    What Is Selena Gomez's and Hailey Beiber's Relationship Like?

    Selena Gomez and Hailey Bieber have had a strained relationship for many years; at least, that is what it appears to be.

    It's no secret that Gomez famously dated Justin Bieber for 8 years before they broke up. Even though many fans loved and continue to love the duo together even affectionately, nicknaming them Jelena, the stars have since admitted that they are happy with their current lives and have moved on.

    Hailey allegedly began dating Justin when he was still romantically involved with Selena (a rumor Hailey has vehemently denied); because of this rumor, media and fans have often called Hailey a homewrecker and make constant comparisons to both ladies.

    The supposed rivalry got so intense that fans even accused Hailey Bieber of making fun of Selena's eyebrows, which Hailey again denied. Selena Gomez then graciously stepped in and asked fans to stop harassing Hailey, adding that she has never condoned bullying.

    Hailey Bieber thanked Selena profusely on Instagram for defending her character; she then stated that the two had been speaking privately and working on resolving the negative narrative between them.
