    What is Heidi Ho? Who is the Founder of Heidi Ho?

    Heidi Ho is a revolutionary plant-based cheese alternative created by culinary entrepreneur Heidi Lovig. In 2011, Heidi Lovig and her partner Lyssa Story founded Heidi Ho with the mission to provide a delicious and sustainable alternative to traditional dairy-based cheese products. Heidi Ho’s innovative approach to vegan cheese has gained popularity among health-conscious consumers and those with dietary restrictions.

    Heidi Lovig, the visionary behind Heidi Ho, is an experienced chef and entrepreneur. Drawing from her culinary background and commitment to organic, locally sourced ingredients, she developed a cheese alternative that addresses the perceived limitations of vegan cheese products.

    Heidi Ho is made from a blend of organic ingredients, including chia seeds, hazelnuts, red potatoes, red bell peppers, carrots, onions, and cashews. With these carefully selected plant-based ingredients, Heidi Ho offers a flavorful and versatile cheese alternative that can be enjoyed by vegans and cheese lovers alike.
