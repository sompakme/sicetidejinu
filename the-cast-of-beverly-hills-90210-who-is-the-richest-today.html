    If there is one teen drama that immediately reminds us of the '90s it's surely Beverly Hills, 90210. The show was created by Darren Star and produced by Aaron Spelling and its cast immediately became international superstars. Unfortunately, in 2000 — after 10 years and 10 seasons — the show wrapped up. Luckily, the show lives on as it resulted in five spinoffs and reboots (Melrose Place, Models Inc, 90210, Melrose Place again, and BH90210).

    Kicking the list off is Tori Spelling who played Donna Martin on Beverly Hills, 90210. Besides this role, Tori is best known for appearing in shows like Mystery Girls and The Help — as well as movies such as The House of Yes, Kiss the Bride, and The Last Sharknado: It's About Time. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Tori Spelling is currently estimated to have a net worth of $1.5 million.
