    Camila Cabello first rose to fame as one of the members of the girl group Fifth Harmony. Fifth Harmony got together on the second season of the competition show The X-Factor in 2012 and became known for songs like Work From Home and Miss Movin' On. Camila Cabello left the group in 2016 and the group went on an indefinite hiatus in 2018.

    Related: Gucci: 10 Of The Most Expensive Items It Has Ever Made, Ranked

    Since leaving Fifth Harmony, each of the former members has gone on to do solo music. Camila has become known for her own music with songs like Havana and Se√±orita,

    a song she did with Shawn Mendes. This pop star may be known for her music, but she's also got a serious sense of style.

    To see 10 of Camila's most expensive looks, keep reading!

    Tonight Show Layering

    Camila didn't just wear a single expensive piece on the Tonight Show. Instead, she layered them up. She wore a $2,100 dress from Fannie Schiavoni over a Saint Laurent jumpsuit covered in Swaorovski crystals  worth over $2,300.

    She performed the song Never Be the Same, the second single from her debut album, in January of 2018, shortly before the release of her album, Camila. This performance of an selection from her debut album had some serious visuals with the screens in the background and her expensive, glittery ensemble only intensified that.

    Jingle Ball Ruffles

    This ruffled mini dress that Camila wore to the 2019 Jingle Ball has a price tag of over $3,000. The dress is from Raisa Vanessa and is from the designer's Spring/Summer 2020 collection. Being a celebrity definitely has its perks, especially when it comes to clothing because Raisa Vanessa's website states that SS20 collection items won't even be shipped to buyers until March of 2020.

    Related: 10 Celebrities Who Throw Themselves The Most Extravagant Birthday Parties

    Even though Camila's outfit was as white and snowy as the weather outside, Camila definitely wasn't looking chilly in this one-shoulder dress. She also performed a few of her songs that night and opened up about songs from her new album, Romance.

    Feeling Blue

    At the MTV VMAs in 2018, Camila walked home with two awards for Artist of the Year and Video of the Year for her song Havana. To accept those awards, Camila wore a gown from designer Oscar de la Renta that cost almost $7,000.

    Even though Camila was wearing a blue designer gown, we doubt she was really feeling all that blue about the evening's events. Along with winning two huge awards at the show that evening, Camila had been nominated for a number more of them. But, Havana missed out on Song of the Year, an award that went to Post Malone for his song Rockstar.

    Mixed Prints

    This dress features an all-over animal print with some gold design details. It's from Versace and Camila wore it for a photoshoot. The price for this flashy dress? over $6,000.

    Related: 10 Craziest Celebrity Dressing Room Requests

    This dress is from Versace and is 100% silk. The fact that it's a designer gown and is made in Italy with such luxurious material definitely explains the pricetag that it has attached to it. It's obvious that whether it's at a red carpet or in a photoshoot, Camila always has to have the finest pieces of clothing.

    Her Havana Video Look

    In her Havana video, Camila channeled a telenovela star in a yellow dress and huge earrings. The dress cost $1,290 while those big earrings from Alessandra Rich cost $480.

    This wasn't the only outfit that Camila rocked in the Havana video, but it definitely is one of the more expensive ones. Along with playing the role of a massive telenovela star in a fictional TV show, Camila also played the role of a young woman with a seriously quirky family who heads out for a night on the town.

    Red Carpet Tulle

    At the American Music Awards, Camila rocked a long braid in her hair and a beige, tulle dress from Oscar de la Renta. The price for this dress? Over $8,000.

    Related: 10 Of The Most Expensive Things Dior Has Ever Made, Ranked

    This dress isn't the only time that Camila has worn Oscar de la Renta on the red carpet. This dress, which is so new that it's not even being shipped to non-A list buyers just yet, and her dress at the VMAs were both by the designer. Other stars that have worn Oscar de la Renta gowns include Taylor Swift, Jessica Alba, and Kerry Washington.

    Candid But Not Casual

    When she was leaving Capital London after doing an interview on the air that morning, Camila looked totally flawless in head-to-toe Valentino. She wore a long wool coat that runs over $4,700, a Valentino dress that isn't available online for purchase, and a handbag and boots each worth thousands.

    This outfit was one that she rocked in October and she took time during the Capital Breakfast show to gush about fellow popstar Shawn Mendes. The two of them have done two songs together and have such cute chemistry!

    Black At Billboard

    When attending the 2017 Billboard Women in Music awards, this popstar went all out. This totally vintage-inspired dress that she rocked on the red carpet is from Dolce & Gabbana and costs over $5,000. This black and white outfit was completed by some pricey accessories, including a handbag and booties from the same designer.

    Related: 10 Most Expensive Fenty Products, Ranked

    Other attendees of the Billboard Women in Music event in 2017 included Kelly Clarkson and Kehlani. At the event, Camila Cabello was presented with the 'Breakthrough Artist' award. Not bad since 2017 was the year before her debut solo album was released!

    Her Eye-Catching Event Look

    At the 2018 Jingle Ball, Camila had heads turning when she paired this simple white crop top with a massive, sparkling tulle skirt from Christian Siriano. This beaded skirt has a price tag of $6,500.

    Along with walking the red carpet in this eye-catching outfit, Camila also performed a few of her songs that evening. A few days before the event, she even posted a picture with Shawn Mendes and teased that the two of them were working together. Was it a clue that Se√±orita was coming?

    Her Skechers Campaign

    Even though the sneakers that Camila wore for this Skechers campaign were only around $50, the dress she was wearing was definitely a lot more expensive. This multi-patterned dress is from Versace and costs just under $4,000.

    Celebrities are always doing major campaigns for other brands. From Ariana Grande's Reebok campaign to Billie Eilish's collab with Freak City, fans are always excited to see their favorite celebrities working with their favorite brands.
