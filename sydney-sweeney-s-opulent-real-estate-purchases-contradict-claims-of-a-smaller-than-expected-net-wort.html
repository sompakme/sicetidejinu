    Sydney Sweeney Has Splurged On Multiple Real Estate Purchases

    Sydney Sweeney has amassed a jaw-dropping net worth since bursting onto the scene with her critically acclaimed performances in smash-hit TV shows like The White Lotus and Euphoria. While current estimates peg her net worth at around $40 million, Sweeney's real estate portfolio hints at a level of wealth that far exceeds these figures.

    Close

    The now 26-year-old has splurged on some pretty opulent properties in recent years. Her first big buy was a stunning 3,200-square-foot mansion in L.A.'s Westwood neighborhood, snapped up for a cool $3 million in 2021. According to Architectural Digest, the multi-million-dollar Tudor-inspired home boasts five bedrooms, four bathrooms, a brick patio, an outdoor fireplace, and a barbecue, all on a lush 0.27-acre lot.

    She told the Associated Press of the purchase in 2021:

    "I always thought that when I turned 18, I’d have all this money and I’d buy back my parents’ house and put them all back together again. And I never was able to, and I never did. So now being able to be in a house is such an incredible, humbling, amazing accomplishment that I still can’t believe I was able to pull off."

    Just a year later, the Euphoria star made an appearance on The Kelly Clarkson Show, where she revealed that she’d bought back her great-grandmother’s house for an undisclosed amount.

    "My great-grandma had this beautiful house, it's a two-bedroom, cute house that they lived in. My grandma was born in it. My mom was born in it, and then as the kids got older, they built on the property another home,” she shared.

    “And when my great-grandpa passed away and my great-grandma got old, they just couldn't afford to keep it anymore...It was a lot. I called up the owners and I said, "I want to buy my great-grandmother's house back." My "grandy" still lives in the house next door. So I'm neighbors with my "grandy" now.”

    Sydney Sweeney’s Most Lavish Real Estate Is A South Florida Mega-Mansion

    With three properties, each purchased for less than $10 million, Sydney Sweeney's real estate portfolio is well within reach for someone with a reported $40 million net worth. However, the Euphoria star is far from finished expanding her real estate portfolio, and her most recent purchase suggests that her net worth might be growing at a far more exponential pace than previously thought.

    According to DailyMail, Sweeney was in the final stages of negotiations to purchase a 7,000-square-foot mansion in South Florida for a whopping $20 million as of June 2024. However, Page Six confirms she purchased it for $13.5 million. Per TMZ, the oceanfront property is loaded with top-of-the-line amenities, including “a heated and chilled infinity pool with a swim-up bar complete with underwater barstools, a chef's kitchen, wine room, an elevator, a home gym, a game room, and a boat launch.”

    Sydney Sweeney Has Been Rather Vocal About Her Financial Struggles In The Past

    Sydney Sweeney’s alleged $13.5 million real estate splurge might come as a bit of a shock to some, considering Sweeney’s previous comments about her financial situation. In July 2022, the Euphoria star roused controversy after she appeared to complain about her financial situation in an interview with The Hollywood Reporter.

    “They don’t pay actors like they used to, and with streamers, you no longer get residuals. The established stars still get paid, but I have to give 5 percent to my lawyer, 10 percent to my agents, 3 percent or something like that to my business manager. I have to pay my publicist every month, and that’s more than my mortgage.”

    She continued:

    “If I wanted to take a six-month break, I don’t have income to cover that. I don’t have someone supporting me, I don’t have anyone I can turn to, to pay my bills or call for help.”

    However, given her meteoric rise in Hollywood, it wouldn't be surprising if those financial woes are now ancient history. Afterall, Sweeney boasts multiple income streams, all with the potential to multiply her net worth several times over within a couple of years.

    Although most of Sweeney's career earnings aren't available, World Celebs estimated that she raked in an estimated $350,000 per episode for her recurring role on Euphoria. The now 26-year-old also reportedly took home a cool $25,000 per episode for season one of The White Lotus, per Forbes.

    Sweeney is also credited as an executive producer on several of her films, including the 2023 romantic comedy Anyone but You, which brought in a whopping $219.6 million at the box office against a modest $25 million budget. Though it remains unclear how much Sweeney took home from the film, it was likely a hefty sum given her dual role in the production.

    The 26-year-old also presumably banks a steady income from her production company, Fifty-Fifty Films. Established in 2020, the company has been involved in producing multiple films, including the 2024 psychological horror, Immaculate and the highly anticipated HBO young adult murder mystery, The Player's Table.
