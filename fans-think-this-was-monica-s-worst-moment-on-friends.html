    Few shows in TV history were are big as Friends was during its peak years on the small screen. Things weren't always smooth behind the scenes, and some guests were tough to deal with, but each week, the show listened to its audience, and it was able to keep its place at the top for years on end.

    The show had amazing characters, and they all made great contributions to the show's legacy. For the most part, they were good people, but all of them had a darker side that came out every now and again.

    Monica Geller might have been nice at times, but fans have pinpointed her worst moment. We have all the details below!

    'Friends' Is An Iconic Show

    From September 1994 to May 2004, Friends was a juggernaut on NBC that cemented its place in history as one of the best and most loved sitcoms of its era. Sure, there were plenty of other amazing things to watch during the '90s, but millions of fans simply could not get enough of those 6 pals living out in New York.

    The cast boasted a ton of young talent, and for a decade, fans got to watch them play iconic characters who all grew up and ventured to different paths in life. The show certainly had its over-the-top moments, but it was relatable on so many levels, which really helped drive its success all those years ago.

    Thanks to the show's writing, the characters felt real, and the performance given by the cast in each episode helped take things to another level. This combo is what keeps people hooked after their first watch.

    While the characters did make the show thanks to having so many amazing moments, the truth is that they all showed some ugliness more than a few times.

    Monica Wasn't The Only With Forgettable Moments

    Well-written characters aren't always good, nor are they always bad. There needs to be somewhat of a dichotomy there, and thankfully, the lead characters on Friends all had some bad moments to help balance out the good.

    For example, Joey is a character that is universally loved, and while he's normal good, he had a moment that stands out as legitimately bad.

    "Joey leaving a disabled woman in the wilderness after burning her wooden leg," a fan pointed out on Reddit.

    Seems a bit out of character, but the bad moments are also part of who he is.

    Chandler is another character that people love, but despite all of his good qualities, he showed some cruelness on the show.

    "Not sure if it's the worst thing ever done by any of them, but one moment that comes to mind is when Chandler has had enough of exercising with Monica and gets her so depressed that she's sapped of all energy and takes a nap. It's an unusually cruel moment from him and honestly a fairly dark scene," another fan highlighted.

    The subject of the characters and their worst moments has focused on each member of the core group, and when it comes to Monica, there is one devilish moment that stands out in the worst way possible.

    "Mm, yeah, trying to adopt Erica's baby (babies!) via lying about who she is...I sympathized with Monica, but it was still very selfish and honestly, I was with Erica when she called her out after they tell the truth. I am glad Chandler was able to talk some sense into Monica, and then we get one of the best Chandler scenes, imho," a Reddit user wrote.
