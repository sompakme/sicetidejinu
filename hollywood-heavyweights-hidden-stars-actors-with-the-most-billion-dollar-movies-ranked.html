    Until fairly recently in the film industry, the concept of a "billion dollar movie" sounded like a far-fetched dream — then James Cameron's Titanic broke the ice, so to speak.

    Not only did this movie shatter the $1 billion mark, it also reached a once thought to be impossible $2 billion gross.

    Titanic took 74 days to become a billion-dollar movie, and another 5,233 days to reach its astonishing $2 billion box office gross milestone.

    It opened the gate for billion-dollar movies.

    A trend that started as recently as 1997 has since become something of a norm, with 53 billion dollar movies at the box office to date.

    After Titanic, it typically took months for films to earn the title of "billion dollar movie" — now, they are hitting that landmark in days.

    The fastest release to become a billion dollar movie was Avengers: Endgame, which surpassed a billion gross in an astonishing five days … yes, in less than a week.

    Many billion dollar movies have come and gone since Titanic, which brings us to another question: which actor has the most billion-dollar movies under their belt?

    Today, we're looking for the answer to that question, exploring which actors have truly conquered the box office, and tallying how many of their projects saw a billion dollar (or higher) return at the box office.
