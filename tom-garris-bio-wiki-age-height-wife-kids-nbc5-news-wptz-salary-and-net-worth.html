    Tom Garris NBC5 News WPTZ

    Garris currently serves as a Multi-Media Journalist, weekend anchor and weekday reporter at NBC5 News WPTZ in Colchester, Vermont. Prior to his work here, he was a reporter as well as a photographer at MyNBC News for a period of 1 year.

    Furthermore, Tom also worked as a News Director at Ithaca College Television. While in Ithaca, he held various roles at the on-campus radio station (WICB-FM) and Ithaca College Television. Furthermore, he was a reporter, anchor, producer, and news director at ICTV.

    During his final semester, he was the anchor and main correspondent for ICTV Reports, a long-form news magazine. In February 2016, Tom and several other students aided NBC Nightly News in covering the South Carolina Republican Primary.

    He is a recent graduate of the Roy H. Park School of Communications at Ithaca College, where he earned a degree in Journalism with a minor in Economics.
