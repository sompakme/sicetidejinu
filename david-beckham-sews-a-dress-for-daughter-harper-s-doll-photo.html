    Daddy of the year! Victoria Beckham shared a sweet photo of hubby David stitching a dress for his 4-year-old daughter Harper’s doll on Instagram on Saturday, March 26.

    “Harper has daddy stitching her dollies dresses!!!” the mom of four, 41, captioned her pic. “We love you @davidbeckham #lovingdaddy #proudmummy #familyfun.”

    In the shot, the retired soccer pro, 40, is pictured sitting on the floor in a gray henley and sweatpants as he focuses on perfecting his sewing skills — possibly to impress his fashion designer wife?

    The hands-on dad recently penned a sweet message for Victoria on U.K. Mother’s Day (March 6).

    “17 years ago our brood started,” David captioned a black-and-white photo of their four kids tending to a garden. “And these little ones have the most amazing mummy that they love so much … And I love her for many reasons but the main one is because she gave me the most amazing children … Happy mama’s day as the little ones would say.”

    The power couple, who tied the knot in 1999, share Harper and three sons, Brooklyn, 17, Romeo, 13, and Cruz, 10. David and the kids supported Victoria at her New York Fashion Week show on Valentine’s Day, where they sat front-row beside Anna Wintour.
