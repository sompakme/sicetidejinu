    Understanding Fenty Beauty Affiliate Commission

    Wondering how much commission you can earn as a Fenty Beauty affiliate? Let’s take a closer look at the commission structure and payout rates.

    As a Fenty Beauty affiliate, you can earn a commission of 4% per sale. This means that for every purchase made through your unique affiliate referral link, you will receive a percentage of the sale as your commission. For example, if someone buys $100 worth of Fenty Beauty products using your link, you will earn $4 in commission.

    It’s important to note that there is a 30-day cookie window for Fenty Beauty affiliates. This means that if someone clicks on your affiliate link and makes a purchase within 30 days, you will still receive the commission for that sale. This allows you to earn commission even if the customer doesn’t make an immediate purchase after clicking on your link.

    Payouts for Fenty Beauty affiliates can be requested once you reach a minimum threshold of $100 in commission. Once you’ve reached this threshold, you can request your payout and receive your earnings. Fenty Beauty offers various payout options to ensure convenience for their affiliates.
