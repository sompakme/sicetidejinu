    What Does Elle Fanning Think Of Paris Hilton?

    W Magazine's annual TV Portfolio enlisted 21 prominent figures in television to pay tribute to their favorite small-screen characters by embodying their personas. Actress Elle Fanning channeled her inner Paris Hilton as she sported an outfit by Paris Hilton's famous character on the reality show Simple Life.

    The outfit included camouflage-printed overalls over a bright pink bikini top, with one side of the overalls pulled down to reveal her underwear. She completed the look with a pink Von Dutch trucker hat, and sunglasses typical of the early 2000s, and to add to the quirky nature of the ensemble, Fanning chose to wear a pair of white pumps, which appeared out of place on the grassy farmland where the photographs were taken.

    She also pays homage to Hilton's pet dog Tinkerbell, who appeared on the show with her carrying a plush dog. She also added a touch of authenticity by sporting silver stickers to simulate Hilton's belly button piercing.

    Fanning's transformation was so effective that she was nearly unrecognizable, adopting a completely different look from her usual appearance. The actress posted a snap of her photo shoot on her Instagram account with the caption, "Chose to dress as THE ICONIC @parishilton in The Simple Life for W’s TV portfolio. #LovesIt." Several fans were quick to comment on phrases such as "iconic" and "looking great" under her post. Many also paid tribute to Fanning's dedication to the theme by using memorable catchphrases, such as "that's hot" and "keep on sliving," which have become synonymous with Paris Hilton's brand.

It was so fascinating hearing in Paris’ documentary how she put on this persona for the reality show. It’s quite clever."

    She also expressed her admiration for Paris Hilton and her reality show, stating that she watches it every few years to relive her childhood admiration for both Hilton and her co-star Nicole Richie.

    Elle Fanning’s Sister, Dakota Fanning Introduced Her To Paris Hilton

    Elle Fanning has nothing but love for his sister Dakota. Their bond strengthened quickly after they realized their shared passion for acting even though their parents were against it at first. Both Paris Hilton and her friend Nicole Richie are adored by the Fanning sisters.

    Elle said in an interview that during the show's run, she and her sister Dakota would act out the scenes and imitate its catchphrases, "My sister and I would watch it and act out the scenes," she said. "I would normally be Nicole Richie, and she would be Paris. We would say ‘That’s hot’ and ‘Loves it.’ Finally, I get to dress up as Paris and have the dog, because Dakota always had the Chihuahua."

    They were big fans of the two famous It Girls back in the 2000s.

    Paris Hilton’s Influence On Elle Fanning's Fashion

    Paris Hilton has had a significant influence on fashion and style, both in the early 2000s and today. Some of how Paris Hilton has influenced fashion are seen in the ways we style these iconic looks the socialite has made fashionable.

  • Popularizing the "celebutante" style: Paris Hilton's early fashion was characterized by her love for luxury and designer brands, as well as her bold and glamorous outfits. This style became known as "celebutante" and helped to popularize the idea of dressing for the paparazzi and the red carpet.
  • Bringing back the mini dress: Paris Hilton was one of the first celebrities to embrace the mini dress trend, which involved wearing short, body-hugging dresses. This trend became very popular in the early 2000s and has continued to be a staple in fashion today.
  • Making oversized sunglasses a fashion accessory: Paris Hilton was often seen wearing oversized sunglasses, which became a popular fashion accessory in the early 2000s. Today, oversized sunglasses are still a popular choice for many people.
  • Popularizing the Juicy Couture tracksuit: Paris Hilton was a big fan of the Juicy Couture tracksuit, which became a symbol of early 2000s fashion. Today, the Juicy Couture tracksuit has made a comeback, and it is once again a popular fashion item.
  • Embracing social media and influencer culture: Paris Hilton has been very successful in using social media to promote her brand and her fashion choices. She has over 13 million followers on Instagram and has used her platform to promote her fashion and beauty products.
