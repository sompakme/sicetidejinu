    Love On The Sidelines was filmed in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. The filming took place in October and November 2015.

    The movie is a twist to a romantic comedy where a struggling fashion designer finds herself with a job as a personal assistant for Danny, a quarterback sidelined with an injury.

    Emily Kinne plays the character of Laurel who doesnt know anything about football and is the assistant of an injured superstar player.

    The plot of the movie rotates around them having a deal and agreeing to work together. The film takes a romantic turn when they both start to like each other and find love on the sideline.

    The duo has a nice, easy-going chemistry that the viewers can enjoy while having popcorn with their loved ones.

    It is a cute movie that shows the trope of “opposites attract” between fashion designers and football players.

    Where Was Love On The Sidelines Filmed?

    Love On The Sidelines movie was filmed in the Vancouver area in Canada. The movie was released on January 16, 2016.

    The film has the most iconic scene of an aerial view of the BC Place Stadium. The shot shows football practice and the characters in the parking lot outside following the practice.

    The trailer to the movie also shows the beautiful cities in Langley, Pitt Meadows, and one of the most beautiful stadiums in Canada Thunderbird Stadium.

    Vancouver

    Vancouver is one of the famous filming locations in Canada due to its beautiful views on every corner of its street.

    It is an extensive film and television production industry and is also known as Hollywood North. It is mainly used as a substitute for other cities while it can be portrayed as any part of the world.

    Most of the scenes in the movie were shot on different streets and restaurants in Vancouver.

    Emily, the film’s main character, posted a tweet while she came to Vancouver for the shooting and she said she loved the vibe there.

    Langley

    The viewers won’t miss Langley as Danny’s house is there in the movie.

    His house in the movie can be found in High Point Equestrian Estates. The area has a lot of fancy houses and structures which are really impressive and perfect for shooting movies.

    According to the website Property Insight, the home boasts 7 bedrooms and 9 baths. The interior seen in the movie was placed by the movie cast to make it feel more natural and have a lavish feel to it.

    Thunderbird Stadium

    The stadium scene shows the shots of the football practice itself and Laurel finding the players in the hallway with the help of a security guard.

    Being a movie related to a footballer it is obvious for the viewers to see the most amazing stadium in Canada.

    The stadium is located in the area of the University of British Colombia.

    BC Place Stadium

    Aerial establishing shot before football practice and the parking lot outside following the practice at the start of the movie shows Danny practicing in the stadium.

    Plot Summary Of The Movie

    The romantic movie features Emily Kinney and John Reardon as the opposite attraction with a hint of comedy.

    The story starts when Emily, who has no prior experience in the sport of football and hopes to be a fashion designer ends up working for superstar footballer Danny as an assistant.

    He is one of the top quarterbacks in the NFL but he got a foot injury during one of his games.

    His injury affected both his attitude and his performance, and was disturbed by it.

    Laurel is having trouble with her career and urgently needs work. She obtains some help as a personal assistant because of her new roommate.

    The duo’s business relationship is shown in the initial phase of the movie.

    He preferred to have a male assistant and Laurel came to her for work and was not welcomed by him.

    To make things worse, she didn’t have any knowledge of football.

    But after working together for a while, they find a love interest in each other with a twist and comedy to draw viewers’ attention.

    Meet The Cast Of Love On The Sidelines

    The movie has a star-studded cast which makes the movie loved by the viewers.

    Emily Kinney As Laurel

    She is a multi-talented American actress and singer who is known for her role as Laurel in the movie love on the sideline.

    Emily plays the role of an assistant to a superstar football player. In the movie, she is seen as an aspiring fashion designer but due to her financial problem has to work as an assistant.

    Kinney being from Nebraska her love for singing and songwriting is obvious. Her album Expired love from 2013 was highly acclaimed by listeners.

    She says the album is about her story and all the things that happened in her past.

    John Reardon As Danny

    John Reardon has put his name in the movie industry as a dynamic and comprehensive actor. Something that the majority of performers can only hope to achieve.

    He began his career in the spotlight at Mount Allison University as an all-star football player. It didn’t take long for Hollywood to take notice of him due to his timeless good looks and athletic build.

    Due to his career as a football player in college, he got the role of a quarterback in the movie love on the sideline. In the movie, he falls in love with his assistant while not liking her at the beginning of the movie.
