    A newly built house in Malibu’s exclusive Point Dume enclave has just popped up for sale with a whopping $24 million price tag. But that ask seems far less surprising when you consider all of the luxe amenities — not one but two pools, a coveted key to a Little Dume Beach and jaw-dropping mountain vistas, just to name a few.

    RELATED: 15 Photos That Prove Alessandra Ambrosio Is Brazil's Greatest Export

    According to Dirt, Tucked away on a gated parcel spanning nearly 2 acres, the museum-like structure was built from the ground up over five years by developer/designer owners Greg and Mica Belzberg. Completed in 2022, the modern architectural-style home was designed by Standard Architecture — the same firm responsible for crafting eight of millionaire fashion mogul James Perse’s retail boutiques, as well as his personal home.

    A total of five bedrooms and eight baths can be found in 8,000 square feet of rambling single-level living space adorned throughout with soaring cedar-clad ceilings, oak and travertine floors framed by Afrormosia wood, clerestory windows, and Crestron and Lutron automation systems. A guesthouse with a separate entry has its own kitchen and private patio warmed by a fire-pit.

    Other highlights include a voluminous great room featuring a combination living/dining area topped by a 13-foot cedar ceiling, and displaying floor-to-ceiling pocketing glass doors flowing seamlessly to the backyard. A fireside family room and casual dining nook with built-in banquette seating connects to the gourmet kitchen, which is outfitted with dual islands and top-tier appliances; and elsewhere is a media room.

    Sequestered off by itself is a posh master retreat sporting an oversized bath decked out with dual vanities, a glassy shower and standalone soaking tub; and outdoors, the resort-like grounds host a lap pool that spills into a larger swimming pool flanked by a pergola hosting a bar, bath, pizza oven and barbecue, along with numerous patios and courtyards ideally suited to al fresco lounging and entertaining. There’s also a three-car garage with additional space for a golf cart and surfboards.

    The property is co-listed by Branden Williams and Rayni Williams of The Beverly Hills Estates, and Chris Cortazzo of Compass.
