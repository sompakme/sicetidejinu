    Viggo Mortensen is a Danish-American actor, author, musician, photographer, poet, and painter.

    Viggo Mortensen was born October 20, 1958, in New York, America. Grace Gamble and Viggo Peter Mortensen Sr. were his parents and have other siblings.

    Viggo Mortensen started and graduated from Watertown High School and later bagged a bachelor’s degree in Spanish studies and politics in 1980 from the St. Lawrence University in Canton.

    Viggo Mortensen returned to moved to Denmark after school to drive trucks and sell flowers but later got back to Spain to start acting.
