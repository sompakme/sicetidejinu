    Why Travis Kelce Didn't Want His Dad Talking To Taylor Swift

    With the camera always on her, fans have witnessed Taylor Swift interact with Travis Kelce's inner circle during football games. While that mostly includes his friends and Patrick Mahomes' wife Brittany, both of Travis Kelce's parents have also been in attendance.

    However, Kelce wished that his father, Ed Kelce, specifically, had not spoken with his new love interest. "If you know dad like I know dad," Kelce began saying on his podcast New Heights.

    "He shouldn't be talking to Taylor Swift," Jason Kelce finished the train of thought.

    Ed Kelce, father of the two football players, was seen with Taylor Swift in a suite at the Chiefs vs. Broncos game on October 12th, 2023. This was the third game Swift had attended to cheer on Kelce.

    Does Travis Kelce's Dad Like Taylor Swift?

    Thankfully, it seems Travis Kelce did not need to worry about his father Ed and Taylor Swift interacting. Ed gave an exclusive interview to People and revealed that he adored the singer and thought she was "very genuine."

    "I'll tell you something very special that I noticed about Taylor the first time I met her," Ed told the outlet. "We're sitting in the suite, she gets up and in the front room, she gets up to go get a drink or something and she starts picking up empty bottles, cans, plates that are scattered around."

    "And I'm just thinking, I don't think she got the diva memo. She didn't get the spoiled musician. She doesn't know how to pull that off. And that really to me said a whole lot."

    Ed had previously told People that when he learned his son was seeing the musical artist, he began doing research on Swift. He wanted to know what kind of person she was, so he watched many of her old interviews. Ed was pleasantly surprised to see how kind-hearted Swift is as a person.

    "She's very smart. I mean, very. That comes through right away."

    How Travis Kelce's Mom, Donna Kelce Feels About Taylor Swift

    Taylor Swift has only briefly interacted with Ed Kelce, but she has spent a lot more time cheering on Travis Kelce with his mother, Donna. Fans of Taylor Swift loved seeing her interact with Donna, and they thought it was incredibly special for them to bond in that way.

    It is unclear, however, how Donna feels about the singer.

    When asked on the Today Show how it was to hang out with Swift in the suite, Donna simply said, "It was okay."

    The short response worried some fans, especially when she later revealed that she was not a fan of Taylor Swift's music before meeting her.

    Since the interview with the Today Show, however, Donna has said some kind words about Swift. It seems she just does not want to speak on her son's relationship considering it is still so new, and would rather the focus be on Travis Kelce's skill as an athlete.

    Travis Kelce And Taylor Swift's Love Story

    The love story between football star Travis Kelce and pop sensation Taylor Swift all began in July 2023, when Kelce brought up the "Anti-Hero" singer on his podcast, hosted with his brother, Jason Kelce, called New Heights.

    "I was a little butthurt I didn't get to hand her one of the bracelets I made for her," Travis Kelce revealed to his brother on the show. Travis made her a friendship bracelet "with my [phone] number on it." Friendship bracelets are a staple of The Eras Tour.

    Travis Kelce certainly managed to capture Swift's attention with the comment, because she later accepted an invitation to a Kansas City Chiefs game. She was seen cheering on Kelce on from a private suite, accompanied by Kelce's mother, Donna Kelce. Swift and Travis were seen leaving the locker room together after this game.

    Swift has since continued to attend Chiefs games, and they have been spotted going on numerous dates. The couple even made a surprise appearance on Saturday Night Live together.

    "They're really happy. They're not saying they're in love yet. But it's obvious to her friends they're heading in that direction," an insider close to Taylor Swift revealed to Us Weekly. "Friends think they're in love."
