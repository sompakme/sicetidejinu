    Kate Hudson is the definition of an “it” girl. The actress has not only wowed Us on the big screen, but also on the red carpet.

    Through the years, the Almost Famous star has served up breathtaking fashion inspiration, transforming every premiere, awards show or Hollywood event into her own personal runway. From sparkling gowns to silk silhouettes and empowering pantsuits — Hudson has proven to be a fashionable force to be reckoned with.

    Her wardrobe is filled with the best labels, including Oscar de La Renta, Thom Browne, Erdem, Rodarte, Carolina Herrera and more — all of which she wears with pride.

    “If I walk past a store and catch a glimpse of something special in the back, I have to go in [because] I get excited,” Hudson told Vogue in a November 2021 interview. “[Fashion] can change your whole mood, brighten your day … I’ve been lucky enough to wear some truly incredible things and build relationships with the artists who created them, and I don’t take that lightly.”

    One standout style moment from the California native came in 2009 when she attended the Met Gala in Stella McCartney. Hudson looked like a shooting star in the gold sequin halter gown that hugged her curves and featured a cutout at the bodice.

    Figure-flattering looks are a go-to aesthetic for the Something Borrowed star. At the 2013 Golden Globes, she turned heads in a skintight number from Alexander McQueen that featured a keyhole neckline with a gold metallic collar. She made another sexy statement at the 2015 ceremony, glistening onto the red carpet in a fitted design by Versace.

    Hudson’s style is versatile. While she often serves up drama in sultry pieces, she also favorites dainty outfits like ruffled dresses and floral skirt. For the 2018 Screen Actors Guild Awards, Hudson looked whimsical in a pink Valentino gown that was adorned with tiny black hearts and featured tiers of mesh fabric.

    Flash-forward to 2022, the Bride Wars actress looked as elegant as ever in a vine-patterned look from Eden that featured satin green straps at the Mill Valley Film Festival. She pulled the look together with pointed-toe pumps and a beige ribbon in her hair.
