    Born as Etuini Haloti Ngata, Haloti Ngata is the current defensive tackle for the Detroit Lions of the National Football League.

    Of Tongan ancestry, Haloti Ngata came from Inglewood California and later studied at the University of Oregon where he played collegiately. In 2006, he was selected by the Baltimore Ravens as the 12th overall pick in the first round of the 2006 NFL Draft. It was actually the first time that the Ravens chose a first round pick for a defense lineman. On July 28, 2006, Haloti Ngata signed a 5-year, $14 million contract with the Ravens.

    Almost a year later, he married his current wife, Christina Adams.
