    Actress and musician Hilary Duff rose to fame in the early 2000s by starring in the popular Disney Channel show Lizzie McGuire. Ever since then, Hilary has been in the spotlight — whether it was through her acting or music projects. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Hilary Duff is currently estimated to have a net worth of $25 million — and today we're taking a look at just how the star earned that money and what she spends it on.

    From earning her millions through brand deals and famous television shows to spending her money on luxurious houses in Beverly Hills as well as expensive skincare — keep on scrolling to find out more about the star's finances!

    10 Earns: Roles In Movies

    We're kicking the list off with the fact that Hilary Duff certainly earned a majority of her net worth through starring in movies. In particular, in the early 2000s, Hilary was a major teen star — and she had lead roles in some of the most iconic movies of the decade. Hilary starred in flicks like Cheaper by the Dozen, A Cinderella Story, Raise Your Voice, and The Perfect Man.

    9 Spends: Spoiling Her Children

    Those who keep up with the former Disney Channel star surely already know that Hilary Duff actually has three children. With her former husband Mike Comrie Hilary has a son named Luca Cruz, and with her current husband Matthew Koma Hilary has two daughters — Violet Banks and Mae James. Of course, considering that she has three children there is no doubt that she spends quite a lot of money on them – whether it's paying for their swimming lessons or buying them expensive toys.

    8 Earns: Roles In Television Shows

    While Hilary does have her fair share of successful movies — the actress is also very well-known for starring in television shows.

    In fact, Hilary rose to fame by starring in Lizzie McGuire and since then she has played important roles in quite a few shows such as the teen drama Gossip Girl and the comedy-drama Younger.

    7 Spends: Luxurious Properties

    Just like most celebs, Hilary Duff is also a fan of big and luxurious properties and considering that she rose to fame as a child star she has certainly gone through her fair share of homes. Today, the actress and musician lives in a luxurious and very chic Beverly Hills house — and she even shared the inside of it for the cameras.

    6 Earns: Releasing Music

    While Hilary Duff nowadays earns most of her money as an actress — the star did have a quite successful career in music in the early 2000s. So far Hilary has released five studio albums — Santa Claus Lane in 2002, Metamorphosis in 2003, Hilary Duff in 2004, Dignity in 2007, and Breathe In. Breathe Out. in 2015. Needless to say that fans are hoping the star releases new music soon!

    5 Spends: Travelling The World

    Those who follow Hilary Duff on social media certainly know just how much the star enjoys traveling the world. Prior to the ongoing coronavirus pandemic, Hilary Duff and her husband Matthew Koma have traveled to quite a few places. Needless to say that once the traveling restrictions ease up the couple is bound to hit the road again!

    4 Earns: Her Own Product Lines And Books

    Just like most other celebs, Hilary Duff has also ventured out into different business areas and over the years she has released quite a few products of her own.

    3 Spends: Skincare And Makeup

    Considering that Hilary Duff is always in the spotlight it certainly doesn't come as a surprise that the star loves splurging on skincare as well as make-up.
