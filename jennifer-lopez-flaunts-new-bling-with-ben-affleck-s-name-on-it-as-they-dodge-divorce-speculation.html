    Jennifer Lopez showed off a new piece in her jewelry collection, which pays homage to her husband, Ben Affleck.

    On Friday, the actress shared a carousel of images on Instagram, but it was the final one that caught the most attention. The image showed JLo topless in the bathtub, with her delicate collection of necklaces on full display. The photo zoomed into Jen’s clavicle, where a silver and diamond necklace spelling “Ben” rested on her neck. She layered it with a larger cross necklace featuring matching diamonds.

    That wasn’t the only serious bling Jennifer was rocking in the photo. She rested her left hand on her chest, showing off a chunky cocktail ring in the shape of a roe plus her engagement ring, which is estimated to be worth at least $5 million.

    Jen And Ben Have Been Packing On PDA In Response To Divorce Rumors

    While JLo’s photo seems like a lovey-dovey tribute to her husband, it comes amid a swirl of divorce rumors about the famous couple. For months, Jen and Ben’s relationship has allegedly been on the rocks, even though they just celebrated their first wedding anniversary.

    Though Jen and Ben haven’t directly responded to the rumors, they’ve been packing on the PDA during public outings, seeming to suggest all is well in their relationship.

    Earlier this month, JLo shared a series of images from her 54th birthday celebration in the Hamptons. Some of the photos included cute snapshots of her and Ben displaying serious PDA while by the pool.

    “Thank you all for the birthday love. Here’s a lil peek into my party,” Jen wrote in her exclusive On the JLo newsletter, where she shared the exclusive photos. “Ben hosted it at our new house with our closest family and friends. All the kids were there, it was a gorgeous day full of sunshine and perfect for a pool party!!”

    More recently, the couple were spotted shopping together, when Jen picked up a cheeky shirt for her husband.

    "Jennifer picked out a T-shirt for Ben that said, 'Woke Up Sexy As Hell Again,'" a source told Entertainment Tonight. "They were both very happy they were super sweet to the staff. They both looked like they were in a great mood.”

    Jen And Ben Remain Committed To Saving Their Marriage

    Though they till appear in the honeymoon phase, a report from last month claimed JLo recently asked Ben for a post-nuptial agreement out of fear their marriage won’t last.

    "Both of them are realists who have been through divorce and don't want to be dealing with a nightmare money battle if they don't make it out of this rough patch," a source told Star Magazine.

    However, the source emphasized that Jen is still committed to saving their relationship, saying she’s still giving this marriage everything she’s got.” The same report added that Ben is open to signing a post-nup in order to keep Jen “happy.”

    Given JLo’s late selfie, it’s clear she’s keeping her husband close to her heart, both literally and figuratively.
