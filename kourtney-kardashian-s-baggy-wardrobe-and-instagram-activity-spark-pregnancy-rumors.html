    As a reality star, Kourtney Kardashian's life is an open book. The star isn't shy about sharing all aspects of her life with the camera. But could she be keeping a family secret under wraps? That's the question fans are asking after Kardashian's recent appearance and social media activity sparked pregnancy speculation.

    The Poosh founder made it well known her desire to have a baby with her husband, Blink-182 drummer Travis Barker. The couple already has a blended family with Kourtney's three kids and Barker's two kids and stepdaughter. But that hasn't stopped the couple from wanting to expand their brood.

    Related: Are Kourtney Kardashian and Travis Barker Still Trying To Have A Baby?

    Kourtney Kardashian's Baggy Wardrobe Sparks Pregnancy Rumors

    Pregnancy rumors have surrounded Kourtney ever since she and Travis got married. Whenever Kardashian was spotted with what appeared to be a baby bump, Kardashian quickly debunked the rumors.

    But that hasn't stopped baby rumors from circulating and they've recently picked up more steam. Kourtney's recent wardrobe choices consist of baggy clothing has many fans convinced she's hiding a baby bump. Last month, the Lemme founder dressed in a black shirt and white overalls when she attended a Blink-182 concert with her stepdaughter Alabama Barker.

    On June 9, Kardashian was again dressed in baggy attire during an Instagram story for her children's last day of school party. The star posed for a photo in black flared pants and an oversized denim jacket.

    Kourtney Kardashian Adds More Fuel To Pregnancy Speculation

    In the past whenever someone questioned if Kourtney was pregnant, she was quick to shut down the idea. Kardashian has explained her previous "baby bumps" as weight gain or the effects of IVF. But so far she's been mum on the recent speculation.

    The star's recent Instagram activity has added more fuel to the baby rumors. According to The Sun, the star shared a Bible passage about childbirth to her Instagram stories. "We know that the whole creation has been groaning as in the pains of childbirth right up to the present time. - Romans 8.22"

    Kardashian's sharing of the scripture has many believing this is another sign that a Kardashian and Barker baby is on the way. However, this could also be Kardashian's way of trolling based on the latest pregnancy speculation.

    Related: Is Kourtney Kardashian Leaving Clues About Being Pregnant With Travis Barker's Baby?

    Kourtney Kardashian Revealed She Stopped IVF Treatments

    Kardashian and Barker's journey to have a baby hasn't been easy. The 44-year-old froze her eggs and underwent IVF treatments, but was unsuccessful. In the Season 3 premiere of The Kardashians, Kourtney shared an update about her IVF journey.

    "We are officially done with IVF," Kourtney revealed in the confessional. "We would love a baby more than anything, but I just really believe in what God has in store for us. If that's a baby, then I believe that it will happen."

    While the couple stopped IVF, they're not giving up on their baby dreams, opting to let nature take its course. If Kourtney and Travis are expecting, then the couple will announce the news when they're good and ready.
